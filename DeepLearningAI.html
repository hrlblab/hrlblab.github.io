Provides students with an understanding of conceptual and practical aspects of models and algorithms used in deep learning. Key topics covered include: basic mathematical tools and machine learning concepts; modern practical deep feedforward, convolutional, and recurrent networks; regularization for deep learning; optimization for learning deep models; practical design methods.
